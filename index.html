About

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at the AI Graduate School, GIST, advised by Prof. Hae-Gon Jeon at the Visual AI Lab. I received a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from GIST in 2021.

My research interests center on aesthetic image and video enhancement, generative models, and multi-modal modeling. I aim to bridge low-level computational photography with high-level generative reasoning to build systems that produce visually compelling, controllable outputs.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to chat about research or potential collaborations.
